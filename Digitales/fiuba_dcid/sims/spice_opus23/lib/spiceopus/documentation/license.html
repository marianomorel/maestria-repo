License

SPICE OPUS is a free analog circuit simulator engine specially suited for optimization tools. It is based on UC Berkeley's SPICE 3f4 and Georgia Tech Research Institute's XSPICE. SPICE OPUS is developed and maintained by the Faculty of Electrical Engineering at the University of Ljubljana, Slovenia.
